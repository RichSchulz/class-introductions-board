# Class Introductions Board

## Project
A one-page site where students introduce themselves to the class.

## Goal
A student enters their **first name**, **major**, **year**, and **general hometown or region**; optionally uploads a **photo**; and sees everyone's cards.

## Hosting & Storage
- **Netlify** for hosting
- **Netlify Functions** for the backend (submit / list entries)
- **Netlify Blobs** for storing entries and uploaded photos

## Style
- Simple and mobile-friendly
- Photos displayed in a neat grid of cards
